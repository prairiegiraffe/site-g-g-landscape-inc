About Us

The Team Behind the Work

G&G Landscape, Inc. is a full-service landscaping contractor headquartered in Gillette, Wyoming. We build landscapes for commercial and residential clients across northeast Wyoming and western South Dakota.

Our Story

Grounded in Gillette

From our office at 5400 Hitt Boulevard in Gillette, Wyoming, G&G Landscape, Inc. serves general contractors, developers, municipalities, and property owners across the Powder River Basin. We build landscapes that hold up in Wyoming's demanding climate.

Our work spans everything from large-scale commercial site grading and irrigation system installation to residential retaining walls and backyard hardscapes. We run our own equipment, manage our own crews, and stay on-site from mobilization through punch list.

The companies and contractors we work with know what to expect from G&G: direct communication, consistent field leadership, and finished work that meets spec. That's the standard, and it doesn't change whether the job is a 100-acre commercial pad or a residential paver patio.

How We Work

What Sets G&G Apart

The difference is in the details — and in who shows up every day.

Single-Source Accountability

One company, one crew, one point of contact. G&G handles grading, irrigation, planting, and hardscapes — no subcontractor handoffs or finger-pointing.

Own Equipment, Own Crews

We run our own iron and employ our own field teams. That means better schedule control, consistent quality, and no gaps between mobilization and completion.

Schedule-Driven Execution

Commercial projects run on tight timelines. G&G coordinates with GCs and project managers to hit milestones and stay out of the way of other trades.

Regional Coverage

Based in Gillette and serving seven communities across northeast Wyoming and western South Dakota — Sheridan, Buffalo, Casper, Douglas, Sundance, and Spearfish.

Built for Wyoming

We know the soils, the weather, and the permitting. Our installations account for freeze-thaw, wind load, and drainage patterns specific to the Powder River Basin.

Commercial & Residential

G&G works at both scales — large commercial and municipal projects as well as residential installations. Same standards, same follow-through.
