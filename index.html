What We Do

Full-Scope Landscaping Services

From raw dirt to finished grounds, G&G Landscape handles every phase of commercial and residential landscape construction.

Site Grading & Prep

Commercial site grading, cut-and-fill operations, and subgrade preparation for construction projects.

Irrigation Systems

Design, installation, and maintenance of commercial and residential irrigation systems built for Wyoming conditions.

Landscape Installation

Complete landscape builds — plantings, mulch beds, boulders, ornamental grasses, and ground cover.

Retaining Walls

Engineered retaining wall systems for grade changes, erosion control, and structural support on commercial sites.

Hardscapes

Patios, fire pits, seat walls, and decorative concrete — functional outdoor spaces that last.

Hydroseeding

Large-scale hydroseeding for rapid vegetation establishment on disturbed land and construction sites.

About G&G Landscape

Built for the Work That Matters

Based in Gillette, Wyoming, G&G Landscape, Inc. is a commercial and residential landscaping contractor. We work with general contractors, property managers, municipalities, and homeowners who need landscape construction done right — on schedule, on spec, and built to hold up.

Our crews handle projects from site grading through final plantings. Whether it's a 50-acre commercial pad, a municipal park, or a backyard retaining wall, G&G brings the equipment, experience, and follow-through to get it done.
