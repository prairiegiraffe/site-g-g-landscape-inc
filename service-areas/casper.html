Serving Casper and Natrona County

Casper is Wyoming's second-largest city, positioned along the North Platte River in Natrona County. The city's commercial corridors, retail centers, healthcare campuses, and residential neighborhoods create steady demand for professional landscape work — and G&G Landscape serves Casper with the full range of services we deliver across the region. Our crews travel south from Gillette on I-25 to handle commercial and residential projects throughout the Casper metro area.

The landscape around Casper is defined by the river corridor, Casper Mountain to the south, and open high-desert terrain in every other direction. That geography creates variable conditions across the city: properties near the river deal with different soil and drainage considerations than those on the benches or along the commercial strips. Wind is constant, winters are cold, and summers bring dry heat. Irrigation systems, proper drainage, and climate-appropriate plant selections are not optional here — they're the difference between a landscape that survives and one that doesn't.

G&G Landscape works with commercial developers, property managers, contractors, and homeowners across Casper. From site grading on a new commercial pad to retaining walls on a hillside residential lot to hydroseeding a large disturbed area, we bring equipment and crews sized for the job.
