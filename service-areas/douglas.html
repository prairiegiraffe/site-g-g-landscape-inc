Landscaping for Douglas and Converse County

G&G Landscape serves Douglas, the Converse County seat, with the same commercial-grade landscaping we deliver across northeast Wyoming. Situated along the North Platte River, Douglas presents specific conditions — alkaline soils, wind exposure, and seasonal temperature swings — that demand landscape solutions built around local reality, not catalog assumptions.

Douglas continues to grow as a hub for energy, ranching, and regional commerce. Whether the project involves site grading for new commercial development, irrigation for a ranch property, or hardscape work for one of the businesses along the main corridor, we bring equipment and crews that match the scale of the job. Our Gillette base puts us within easy reach of Douglas for both project execution and ongoing maintenance.

From residential sod installation to large-scale hydroseeding for land reclamation, every project in the Douglas area gets planned around the soil, the water table near the river, and the wind patterns that define Converse County. We handle the full scope — grading, drainage, planting, hardscapes — so you work with one contractor from start to finish.

Our Services in Douglas

Every service we offer in Gillette is available to Douglas-area clients. Here is what we bring to Converse County.

Commercial-Site Grading & Prep

Precision earthwork to establish proper grades and drainage before construction begins.

Irrigation Systems

Landscape Installation

Complete landscape build-outs using plants, trees, and materials suited to Wyoming conditions.

Retaining Walls

Engineered walls that hold soil in place and manage grade changes on sloped properties.

Hardscapes

Durable stone, concrete, and paver installations built to handle heavy Wyoming freeze-thaw cycles.

Tree Planting
