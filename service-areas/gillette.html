Gillette Is Our Home Base

G&G Landscape operates out of our office on Hitt Boulevard in Gillette, right in the heart of Campbell County. This is where our crews start every morning and where every project gets planned. As the hub of the Powder River Basin, Gillette has a landscape shaped by the energy sector — commercial yards, industrial sites, municipal properties, and growing residential neighborhoods all need professional ground work to handle the demands of this high-plains environment.

The climate here is direct: semi-arid conditions, wind that doesn't quit, and winters that test everything installed in the ground. That means irrigation systems need to be built for efficiency, plant selections have to tolerate drought and cold, and grading has to account for fast-moving runoff. We handle all of it — from large-scale commercial site prep for energy-sector facilities to residential sod installation and backyard hardscapes.

Whether you're developing a new commercial lot along the bypass, maintaining a property near Cam-Plex, or building out a residential yard in one of Gillette's expanding subdivisions, G&G Landscape delivers the full scope of landscape construction and installation services right here in town.
