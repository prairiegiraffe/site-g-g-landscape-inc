Our Services

What We Build

G&G Landscape provides 12 core landscaping services — from site grading and earthwork through irrigation, plantings, hardscapes, and finished grounds.

Full Service List

Commercial & Residential Landscaping

Every service G&G Landscape offers across our seven-area region in Wyoming and South Dakota.

Commercial-Site Grading & Prep

Cut-and-fill operations, rough and finish grading, subgrade compaction, and site preparation for commercial construction.

Irrigation Systems

Design, installation, and maintenance of rotor, spray, and drip irrigation systems with smart controller integration.

Landscape Installation

Complete landscape builds — trees, shrubs, ornamental grasses, perennials, mulch beds, boulders, and ground cover.

Retaining Walls

Engineered block, natural stone, and segmental retaining walls for grade changes, terracing, and structural support.

Hardscapes

Natural stone, decorative concrete, fire pits, seat walls, and outdoor living spaces built for year-round use.

Artificial Turf Installation

Synthetic turf systems for commercial properties, play areas, pet runs, and low-maintenance landscape zones.

Sod & Lawn Installation

Large-format sod installation for instant lawn establishment on commercial and residential properties.

Hydroseeding

Hydraulic mulch seeding for rapid revegetation on slopes, disturbed areas, and large-scale erosion control projects.

Tree Planting

Specimen tree selection, transport, and installation — from container stock to large-caliper trees moved by tree spade.

Drainage & Erosion Control

French drains, catch basins, swales, rip-rap channels, and erosion control blankets for stormwater management.

Paver Patios & Walkways

Interlocking concrete pavers and natural stone for patios, walkways, driveways, and commercial pedestrian areas.

Water Features & Ponds

Custom water features, decorative ponds, recirculating waterfalls, and fountain installations for commercial properties.
